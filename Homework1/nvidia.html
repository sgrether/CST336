The World's Most Advanced Gaming GPU Architecture



The Geforce GTX 1080ti uses Pascal archictecture, has an 11GB GDDR5X frame buffer,

11Gbps memory speed, and a boost clock of 1582MHz. It has 3584 Nvidia CUDA Cores.

The GTX 1080ti supports Simultaneous Multi-Projection, VR, Ansel, G-SYNC, GPU Boost 3.0.

It supports a maximum digital resolution of 7680x4320 @ 60Hz and has HDMI 2.0b and DP 1.4 conectors.

This card requires 250W and it's recommended to use a power supply of atleast 600W.

The 1080ti requires a 6-pin and an 8-pin connecter.
